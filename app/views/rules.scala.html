Rules

There are 3 phases in the game.

  1. Place
  2. Move
  3. Fly
Before the game starts a coin has to be tossed to decide who will play white - white then moves first.
White has to place a stone on a free point of its choice. Then black has to place a stone. This continues until all 18 stones have been placed.
Now the player can move a stone to a free adjacent point along the lines
When a player has only 3 stones left he can move a stone to any free point on the board

Case of mill

If 3 stones of one colour form a line the player can remove a stone of his opponent which isn't in a mill (If all the stones are in a mill this rule doesnt apply)
The stones remain outside the board and cant be placed back on it

Game ending

The game ends if one player has only 2 Stones left. The other player wins the game
